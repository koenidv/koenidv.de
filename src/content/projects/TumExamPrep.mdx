Driven by the nonexistence of exam preparation content for Strategic & International Management (SIM) at TUM, I created TUM Exam Prep.

This web app offers more than 20 test exams with hundreds of close-to-life multiple-choice questions, in exchange for a small fee handled through Stripe.
No account is necessary to minimize friction, however users can opt to sync their progress using a Google sign-in.

To build the full platform including payments, device sync and content in less than half a day, I used Lovable and Antigravity, with human-written code only for more complex or security-relevant parts.
